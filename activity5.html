Why your body needs exercise

Your body is like a supercar the more you use it the stronger it gets! Kids who move for 60 minutes every dayare happier, sleep better, and do better at school.

Dancing, swimming, football, yoga anything thats gets your heart beating faster counts. Keep moving and have fun!

Quick health tips

  • Eat 5 fruits and veggies everyday
  • Go out to the park and play footabll
  • If you can go to the gym
  • Dont study for more than 3 hours straight, take breaks
  • Do stretches every morning
